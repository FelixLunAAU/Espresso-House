About us

Espresso House history dates back to 1996 when our very first café was opened by Denitsa Chaymeek at Kyrkogatan in Lund. What started out as a small coffee shop inspired by both the Italian coffee culture and the American café tradition, is today a leader in the Nordic region with 3 coffee shops, 40 baristas and 20,000 visitors daily. In October 2018, Espresso House opened its first coffee shop in Hamburg, Germany. Today we are divided into five countries; Sweden, Norway, Finland, Denmark and Germany.

In addition to our full espresso and brew bar menu, we serve fresh made-to-order breakfast and lunch sandwiches, as well as a selection of sides and salads and other good stuff.

"We want to put the coffee in the center of our restaurants, with attention to every little detail" It's all in the details

- Founder and Barista, Denitsa Chaymeek

Denitsa making a Cappuccino in the first Espresso House location in Lund.

Our Locations

At Espresso House we like to keep track of things, therefore we do not believe in franchises. We want everyone who visits us to immediately feel that they are at an Espresso House - in terms of both coffee and staff and the environment. We currently have three different Cafés all located in the South of Sweden.
